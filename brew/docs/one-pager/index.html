The loop

four small moves, one bag at a time

i
Set your gear
Pick your espresso machine and grinder. The grinder's native scale shapes every slider.
ii
Log a bag
Snap the label — on-device OCR pulls brand, origin, roast, weight into the form.
iii
Rate each brew
Espresso, cortado, iced latte, cappuccino. One rating per drink — update it when it's better.
iv
Watch it tune
After a few ratings, new bags get a starting grind suggestion based on your own history.
