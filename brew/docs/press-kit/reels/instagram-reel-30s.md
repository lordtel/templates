## Production notes

- **Hook rule:** no text or logo at 0:00 — just the espresso pull. The problem lands at 0:02.
- **Cuts:** ~3 s per scene. Use a jump cut at 0:02 (espresso → blank notes) for contrast.
- **Music:** low-key lo-fi or light jazz underneath; duck to –18 dB under the VO.
- **Captions:** auto-generate from the VO above; keep styling consistent with the on-screen text.
- **End card:** hold the last frame 0.5 s past the VO so the URL is readable before the loop.
- **CTA overlay (optional):** add a swipe-up / link-in-bio sticker at 0:27 pointing to crema.live.
