## Tone & delivery notes

- **Warmth, not hype.** Read it like you're explaining something interesting at a café, not pitching to investors.
- **Slow on the six-axis line.** "Bright. Bold. Rich. Strength. Long pull. Dose." — give each word its own beat, matching the dots popping in. About 1 second per word.
- **Drop the volume slightly on the last line.** "Your espresso, dialed in." should land soft, almost like a sigh. Not a sales close.
- **The "…" between sentences are real pauses.** Let the visual breathe.

## If you'd rather generate it with AI

Paste the **Primary script** into ElevenLabs / Murf / Play.ht. Suggested settings:

- **Voice profile:** Warm, conversational, mid-range. Avoid news-anchor or tutorial voices.
- **ElevenLabs presets that work well:** *Brian* (calm, warm) or *Charlotte* (soft, deliberate). Stability ~50, Style 30.
- **Pace:** 0.95× normal (slightly slower than default).
- **Pause settings:** Add SSML `<break time="500ms"/>` after each `…` and at the start of "Bright."

Trim breaths in Audacity, match it to the 30 s reel in CapCut, drop a soft lo-fi or jazz bed at –20 dB beneath, export 1080×1920.
