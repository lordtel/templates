# Crema — Instagram strategy

A sustainable, single-creator-friendly plan for **@crema** on Instagram. Optimised for a niche specialty-coffee audience and a brand that's quiet, crafted, and slightly opinionated.

---

## 1 · Goals & metrics

**Primary goal:** drive installs of crema.live (visit → guest mode → first bag logged).

**North-star metric:** weekly link clicks on `crema.live` from bio + sticker links.

**Secondary metrics (in priority order):**

| Metric                | Target after 90 d | Why                              |
| --------------------- | ----------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Saves per post        | ≥ 30              | Save = useful, signals to algo   |
| Profile visits        | ≥ 200/week        | Funnel top                       |
| Bio link clicks       | ≥ 40/week         | Bottom of funnel                 |
| Followers             | ≥ 1,000           | Vanity, but unlocks features     |
| Story replies         | ≥ 5/week          | Real signal of engagement        |

Don't chase likes. Saves and link clicks matter more for an app.

---

## 2 · Posting cadence

**Realistic for one person, indefinitely sustainable:**

- **2 feed posts / week** — Tuesday + Friday (highest engagement windows for specialty coffee accounts)
- **1 reel / week** — Sunday evening or Monday morning (algorithm pushes reels harder Mon–Wed)
- **3–5 stories / week** — ad-hoc, low effort
- **0 IGTV / lives** — skip until you have an audience

**Total:** 3 posts + 5 stories per week. Quality over quantity, always.

**Don't over-post.** Posting 5×/week with mediocre content is worse than 2×/week with sharp content.

---

## 3 · Content pillars (rotate)

Keep four pillars on rotation so the feed feels varied without you scrambling for ideas.

| Pillar                  | Format          | Cadence         | Examples                                           |
| ----------------------- | --------------- | --------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Feature showcase**    | Reel / carousel | 1× / week       | Taste profile reel · OCR scan · dial-in companion  |
| **Espresso education**  | Carousel        | 1× / week       | "Why coffee needs to rest 7 days" · Ratio explained · Grind sizes |
| **Bag-of-the-week**     | Story + post    | 1× / week       | Tag the roaster (%Arabica, La Cabra, Drop, April), share dialed-in recipe |
| **Behind-the-scenes**   | Story           | 1–2× / week     | Dev updates, what's-new popup, sneak peeks         |

This split gives you ~60% product, ~40% lifestyle/community — which is what specialty coffee audiences engage with.

---

## 4 · Format mix

| Format         | When                                  | Why                                                   |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Carousel** (10 slides) | Education, bag-of-the-week    | Highest dwell time → algo love. Add a "swipe →" hook. |
| **Reel** (≤ 30 s)        | Feature showcases             | Reels reach > posts in 2026.                          |
| **Single image**         | Rare — only when truly iconic | Lowest reach. Skip unless the photo is genuinely beautiful. |
| **Story**                | Tagging roasters, polls, AMA  | Disappears in 24 h, low pressure.                     |

**Story sticker rotation:**
- "Tag your roaster" sticker → user-generated mentions
- Poll: "What's your dial-in struggle?"
- Q&A: "Ask me anything about espresso"
- Link sticker → crema.live

## 5 · Hashtag strategy

Use **10–15 tags** per post — more dilutes and looks spammy. Mix sizes for reach + relevance.

### Always-on bundle (save in Notes, paste every post)

```
#specialtycoffee #espresso #homeespresso #thirdwavecoffee
#coffeegram #manualbrew #baristalife #coffeenerd
#cremalive #espressoathome
```

### Add 3–5 contextual tags per post

**Equipment-themed posts:**
```
#decentespresso #lamarzocco #ecm #profitec #niche #timemore
```

**Roaster-themed posts:** tag the roaster's username + 2–3 of:
```
#singleorigin #lightroast #washedcoffee #naturalprocess
```

**Education posts:**
```
#coffeescience #brewscience #coffeeratio #grindsize #dialingin
```

**Brand-tag (own):**
- `#crema` and `#cremalive` — start using to seed UGC

### Avoid

- Generic mega-tags (`#coffee` 500M+ posts → invisible)
- Banned/shadowbanned tags (check IG help if unsure)
- Spammy combos like `#instagood #love #photooftheday`

## 6 · Captions

### Hook in line 1

The first 80 characters show in feed before "more". Front-load the punch.

✓ "Five-star ratings don't tell you much."
✓ "Stop re-dialing the same bag twice."
✗ "Hey friends! Today I want to share with you something exciting…"

### Voice (from `copy.md`)

- Warm, opinionated, slightly contrarian
- Use sentence fragments. Real coffee people talk in short bursts.
- No emojis except sparingly: ☕ 🫘 in caption ends are OK
- Sign off with `crema.live` or "link in bio" only when there's a real CTA

### Caption template

```
[Hook — 1 line, ≤ 80 chars]

[2–4 lines of context. Why this matters. What you do differently.]

[CTA: "Try it on crema.live", "Tag your roaster below", "Save for your next dial-in"]

—
[10–15 hashtags, no @mentions inline — they go in caption end or first comment]
```

## 7 · Growth tactics that actually work

In rough priority order:

1. **Tag roasters when you feature their bags.** Highest-leverage move. They reshare to story → you reach their audience. Aim for 1 tagged-roaster post / week.

2. **Comment thoughtfully on 5 specialty roaster posts / day.** Not "🔥🔥" — real comments that add. They check who you are. Profile visits climb.

3. **Story replies > comments.** Drop replies to roaster stories with 1 line of context. DMs are how relationships start.

4. **Reel hooks in 1 s.** Most people scroll past the title card. Open on motion, then layer text. (The taste-profile reel does this — chart starts drawing immediately.)

5. **Pinned posts.** Pin 3 of your best to your grid: best feature reel, best educational carousel, "what is Crema" post. New visitors decide in 6 seconds.

6. **Bio CTA.** "Espresso journal · log every shot · crema.live ↓" with a clear arrow to the link.

7. **Cross-post to threads / TikTok.** Same content, 3× the reach. Repurpose, don't create new.

8. **Don't pay for ads until 1K followers + clear winning post.** Boost what already works rather than testing cold.

## 8 · Weekly schedule (template)

| Day         | What                                        | Effort       |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------- | ------------ |
| **Mon**     | Reel goes live (was scheduled Sun night)   | 0 (scheduled) |
| **Tue**     | Feed post (carousel education or showcase)  | 30 min        |
| **Wed**     | 1–2 stories (BTS, dev update, poll)         | 10 min        |
| **Thu**     | Engagement-only day (reply, comment 30 min) | 30 min        |
| **Fri**     | Feed post (bag-of-the-week, roaster-tagged) | 30 min        |
| **Sat**     | 1 story, weekend mood                       | 5 min         |
| **Sun**     | Plan + schedule next week's reel            | 60 min        |

**Weekly time budget:** ~3 hours. Sustainable with a day job.
